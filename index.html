From Directors Desk

"My journey to setting up the 'Blue Dot Art Gallery' has taken around 2 decades in the incubation."

The Vision of opening this gallery arose to fill up the disconnect in the Art Market. The Vision of opening such a space where a formal Art Gallery and space for teaching the "language of Art" is combined.

The genesis of this desire to spread Art literacy arose from my own loss at the appreciation of the great works of Art, "masterpieces of Art", or "traditional", folk, or vernacular Art because of a lack of tools to read the 'language of Art'.

To this end, I persuaded my Master in Fine Arts from Baroda in Art History. Fine Art's faculty in Baroda was a space for exploring Art in all its facets with inspiring teachers and creative students.

While I did my "Managing the Art" - a short MBA program on Arts management at the Asian Institute of Management, Manila. I envisaged this concept of an Art gallery teaching Art History and aesthetics of India and the West. A gallery with a focus on learning and educating patrons to be informed buyers.

Over the years of teaching and interacting with students of NIFT (Mumbai and Gandhinagar). I have been inspired by the feedback from the ex-students who have motivated me to share the world of Art.

My granddaughters are the ones who helped manifest this gallery. I hope to reach out to all "Art lovers" who desire to be Art 'literate'. How to shed prejudices and appreciate Art with its changing meaning.
